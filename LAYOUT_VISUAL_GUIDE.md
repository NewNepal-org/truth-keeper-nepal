## Responsive Behavior

### Mobile (< 768px)
- **Layout 1:** Hero image scales down, overlay readable
- **Layout 2:** Stacks to single column, image at top
- **Layout 3:** Cards stack vertically, maintains card structure
- **Layout 4:** Image moves to top, metadata stacks

### Tablet (768px - 1024px)
